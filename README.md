# Privacy Policy for Khana Ready

**Last Updated: April 19, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Khana Ready ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Khana Ready (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you register and use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Profile Information**: Student ID, college/institution name, profile picture
- **Authentication Data**: Google Sign-In credentials (email, name, profile picture)
- **Payment Information**: Bank account holder name (for payment verification only - we do not store credit card or bank account numbers)

### 2. Order Information

- Order details (items, quantities, prices)
- Order history and transaction records
- Shop preferences and favorites
- Delivery/pickup information

### 3. Device Information

- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- App version and usage data

### 4. Location Information

We may collect approximate location information to:
- Show nearby cafes and shops
- Improve service delivery
- Provide location-based features

### 5. Images and Media

- Shop and menu item photos uploaded by shop owners
- Profile pictures (optional)

### 6. Notifications Data

- Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens for push notifications
- Notification preferences and settings

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 1. Service Delivery
- Process and manage your orders
- Facilitate communication between students and shop owners
- Send order status updates and notifications
- Manage user accounts and authentication

### 2. Payment Processing
- Verify payment transactions
- Match payment account names with orders
- Prevent fraud and unauthorized transactions

### 3. App Improvement
- Analyze app usage and performance
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features and improvements
- Enhance user experience

### 4. Communication
- Send order confirmations and updates
- Notify about order status changes
- Provide customer support
- Send important service announcements

### 5. Security
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Ensure platform security and integrity

## Data Storage and Security

### Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase services for:
- **Firebase Authentication**: Secure user authentication
- **Cloud Firestore**: Encrypted data storage
- **Firebase Storage**: Secure image and media storage
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: Push notifications

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Secure HTTPS connections
- Data encryption
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Secure coding practices

### Data Retention

- Active user data is retained while your account is active
- Order history is retained for 2 years for record-keeping
- Deleted accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- You can request data deletion at any time

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### We Share Information With:

1. **Shop Owners**: When you place an order, shop owners receive:
   - Your name and contact information
   - Order details
   - Payment account name (for verification)

2. **Service Providers**: We may share data with:
   - Firebase/Google Cloud Platform (hosting and infrastructure)
   - Analytics providers (anonymized data only)

3. **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by:
   - Law enforcement or legal process
   - Protection of our rights and safety
   - Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data for marketing purposes
- Provide your information to advertisers

## Your Rights and Choices

### You Have the Right To:

1. **Access Your Data**: Request a copy of your personal information
2. **Update Information**: Modify your profile and account details
3. **Delete Account**: Request permanent deletion of your account and data
4. **Opt-Out of Notifications**: Disable push notifications in app settings
5. **Data Portability**: Request your data in a portable format
